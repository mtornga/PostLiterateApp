# Google Play Store Listing - Read This For Me

## Short Description (76 characters)
Snap a photo of any text to hear it read aloud. No account needed. Private.

## Full Description

**Read This For Me** helps you understand written text by reading it aloud.

**How it works:**
• Point your camera at any text — menus, forms, signs, mail, medicine labels
• Tap the clipboard button to hear the words spoken aloud
• Tap the brain button to hear a simple explanation of what the text means

**Built for privacy:**
• No account required — just open and use
• Your photos are never saved
• No tracking, no history, no one knows what you read
• Everything is forgotten after it speaks

**Perfect for:**
• Reading menus at restaurants
• Understanding medical paperwork
• Decoding official letters and forms
• Navigating signs and notices
• Anyone who wants text read aloud quickly

**Features:**
• High-quality natural voice (Google Cloud Text-to-Speech)
• Adjustable playback speed — slower or faster to match your pace
• "Explain" mode simplifies complex text into plain language
• Choose short, medium, or long explanations to fit your needs
• Works in any language supported by Google Vision

**Simple by design:**
This app has no settings, no tutorials, and no learning curve. Open it, point at text, and tap. That's it.
