Privacy Policy for Read This For Me

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

1. Overview

"Read This For Me" is designed to assist with reading and understanding text through visual capture. Your privacy is paramount.

Key Point: We do not store your images or text. Everything is processed in real-time and immediately forgotten.

2. Information We Process

  • Camera Images: We use your camera to capture text. These images are transmitted to Google Cloud Vision API for text recognition (OCR).
  • Text Data: Recognized text may be sent to Google Gemini AI to provide summaries and explanations when you use the "Explain" feature.
  • Local Data: Daily usage limits are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

3. Data Processing

  • We do not store your images or text on our servers.
  • Data is processed in real-time and discarded after the request is complete.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) like names, emails, or locations.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to process your requests:

  • Google Cloud Vision - For optical character recognition (OCR)
  • Google Gemini AI - For text explanation and simplification
  • Google Cloud Text-to-Speech - For converting text to audio
  • Firebase Cloud Functions - For secure backend routing

By using the app, you agree to their respective privacy policies.

5. Security

All communication between the app and our backend is encrypted via HTTPS. Your data is protected in transit.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
